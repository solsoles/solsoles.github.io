    About Us

    We are a group of students from the Medina County Career Center in Medina Ohio. We wanted to start a company that has never been done before, so we thought of something that we were troubled by. We liked the idea o fa fitbit but wanted something more unique so we can up with SOL.

    What We Stand For.

    At SOL, we are dedicated to empowering individuals to take control of their athletic and orthopedic health. Our mission is to create a world where every person can maximize their physical potential while reducing the risk of injury and chronic conditions

    We understand that your body is your most valuable asset, and we are committed to helping you make informed decisions about your health and well-being. Whether you're an avid athlete or simply someone who wants to improve their overall health, SOL is here to support you on your journey.
